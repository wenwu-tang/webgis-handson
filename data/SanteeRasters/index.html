Raster Datasets for USDA Forest Service Santee Experimental Forest

This dataset includes 9 raster files in GeoTIFF format that cover the Santee Experimental Forest.

Data Source

The source of these GeoTIFF raster datasets is the USDA NAIP Public Image Service (USDA: United States Department of Agriculture; NAIP: National Agriculture Imagery Program).

Source website: https://naip-usdaonline.hub.arcgis.com/

Year of Data Collection

2023
